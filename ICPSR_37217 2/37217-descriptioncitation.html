PFAS Results in Community Water Supplies (CWS) in Michigan, 2018 (ICPSR 37217)

Principal Investigator(s): Michigan. Department of Environmental Quality

Project Description:

Summary:

These data are publicly available as part of Michigan's Open Data Portal and are updated regularly by the state.

This dataset contains information on per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) in community water supplies (CWS) across Michigan counties.

Citation:

Michigan. Department of Environmental Quality. PFAS Results in Community Water Supplies (CWS) in Michigan, 2018. Ann Arbor, MI: [distributor], 2019-01-04. https://doi.org/10.3886/ICPSR37217.v1

Subject Terms: community health, environment, public health, public utilities, water pollution

Geographic Coverage: United States, Michigan

Smallest Geographic Unit: county

Scope of Project:

Time Period(s): 2018

Date of Collection: 2017-09-28 -- 2018-06-26

Data Collection Notes: The data were downloaded from Michigan's Open Data Portal PFAS Results in CWS page on November 9, 2018. The data are updated regularly by the State of Michigan; if these data have been updated since the download date, the most up-to-date version of the data will be available via the link provided.

Methodology:

Time Method: Cross-sectional

Universe: Community water supplies in Michigan in 2018.

Unit(s) of Observation: water sample

Data Type(s): survey data

Description of Variables: Names of county and public water supply, population served, sampling date and number, raw water or treated drinking water, per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS), perfluorooctanoic acid (PFOA), and perfluorooctanesulfonic acid (PFOS) parts per trillion (ppt).
